Manthan 2025

Manthan 2025

“Manthan 2025” is a 3 days annual fest& reflects the vibrant confluence of athleticism and artistic expression that embodies the spirit of our institution. Over three exciting days, “Manthan 2025” will feature a diverse array of activities that blend competitive sports, intellectual challenges, creative pursuits, and cultural celebrations. The event includes track and field sports, exhibitions of innovative projects, quizzes, photography, cultural performances like singing and dancing, traditional art competitions, and much more. This dynamic platform fosters creativity, teamwork, and a deep appreciation for cultural heritage, ensuring a holistic experience for all participants. Each day will offer unique opportunities for physical engagement, mental stimulation, and artistic expression. exemplifies our commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals by creating a vibrant platform that combines athletic vigor with cultural finesse.

Special in Manthan

The event will be a magnificent showcase of the talents of our students in sports and artistic endeavors. Such events play a crucial role in the growth of engineering students, fostering teamwork, leadership, and creative thinking. They promote a healthy lifestyle, alleviate academic stress, and build a sense of community among students. Moreover, they provide opportunities to exhibit skills, celebrate cultural diversity, and enhance personal growth. Events like these help shape students into value-driven individuals who can contribute meaningfully to society and nation-building, aligning with our vision of developing our institute as a resource center for the community. We anticipate over 5,000 participants and attendees during the three days, including students from RVIT and other engineering institutions, as well as nearby schools and colleges, along with parents, guardians, and family members.

Campus Society

Our society “Sudeshna Educational Charitable Society” was established in 2009 under the Societies Act 1950 and registered under the provisions of The U.P Public Act,1950. The Society is a renowned educational society devoted to the cause of spreading education in the region. The society started R V Institute of Technology (RVIT) & R V Institute of Pharmacy (RVIP), Bijnor to serve the purpose of imparting technical education to both boys and girls of the urban and rural areas. The primary objective of the society is to express the philosophy and essence of engineering for progress and prosperity of mankind. The educational society inscribes waves of knowledge in the entire region thereby acting as the radiating center. The institute acts as a source of process of learning and thereby enabling the students to attain new horizons of progress in their lives.

Vision

To transform young minds into technocrats, globally competitive, highly- skilled with positive mind set, who live with human values in full respect of the co-existence.

Mision

The mission is to impart quality knowledge and educate its students not only in the domain of technology but also in human values so that they can develop into a sound technocrat and a better human being ready to progress in life. We are dedicated to provide such environment that combines rigorous academic studies and the excitement of exploring new innovative ideas with the support of prominent faculties, highly qualified guest lecturers from different field of expertise. We provide value education to our students to help them develop the ability and passion to work wisely, creatively and effectively.

Magic of Manthan

Get ready to experience three thrilling days of creativity, competition, and celebration! At Manthan 2025, we bring together talent, innovation, and endless excitement. Each day is packed with unique activities designed to entertain, inspire, and unite our RVIT family. From high-energy performances to mind-bending challenges – there’s something for everyone! Let’s dive into the day-wise journey and celebrate the spirit of togetherness.
